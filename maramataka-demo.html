Maramataka
Te Marama · Moon Dreaming
Maramataka is a living Māori lunar calendar — a daily companion guided by the ancient wisdom of the moon. Each of the 29 nights carries a name, a meaning, and an invitation to reflect.
🌕
Live Moon Clock
Real-time clock showing tonight's lunar phase, drawn accurately to the sky above you.
📅
Maramataka Calendar
Monthly view with Māori phase names for every day. Tap any day to see its guidance and add appointments.
✦
Wawata Reflections
Each phase carries a wellbeing reflection and a personal question, inspired by the maramataka tradition.
🗓
Appointments
Add and save appointments to any day, colour-coded and stored on your device.
Wawata — Moon Dreaming
The reflections in this app are inspired by the maramataka wellbeing tradition as brought to life by Dr Hinemoa Elder in her book Wawata — Moon Dreaming (Penguin NZ, 2022). Dr Elder is of Te Aupōuri, Ngāti Kurī, Te Rarawa, Ngāi Takoto and Ngāpuhi nui tonu.
